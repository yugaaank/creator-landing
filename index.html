80% Increase in Monthly Clients for a Veterinary Practice via AI Call Bot
Location / Africa
Client / Hagai Avisar  |  Independent Veterinarian  |  AI Call Automation
  • Problem: The clinic relied on manual call handling — missed calls during consultations and surgeries, delayed responses, no automated lead capture, and an inefficient booking process resulting in lost clients.
  • Solution: An AI-powered call bot was deployed to automatically answer every call, collect client information (name, contact, pet concern), handle appointment inquiries, capture leads, and remain available 24/7 including off-hours.
